Where is the Future?

世上任何有价值的东西与有意义的事，都需要用辛劳、痛苦与煎熬换取。此生，我从未羡慕过任何安稳无忧的人，但我羡慕那些遭遇困苦但能从容应对的人。
Anything valuable and meaningful in the world needs to be exchanged with hard work, pain and suffering. In this life, I have never envied anyone who is safe and secure, but I envy those who can handle difficulties calmly.
